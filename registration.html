Registration Info

The Division C (high school) tournament being held on February 15, 2025 is now open for registration by coaches!

  • The fee per team is $85.
  • Invoices will be provided at a later date.
  • Be sure to check the Tournament Info page for important forms and other information.
  • Registration is rolling and capped at 20 teams.
  • After the registration limit has been reached, teams will be placed on a waitlist.
  • A school is limited to two teams, but is up to our discretion.
  • Teams from Vermont and New Hampshire will be prioritized in registration.
